    Born from a quest for alternatives closer to the home and heart than eating out and
    ordering in, Swadh Kitchens is all about door delivering home-cooked meals and
    homemade delicacies. Each plate of food served through Swadh Kitchens is prepared
    using fresh, locally sourced produce in a home kitchen just like yours with authentic,
    regional recipes by real people with a passion for cooking.
    The food is procured from our network of home chefs, who use only garden fresh
    ingredients to prepare decadent dishes in home kitchens that are compliant with
    sanitization guidelines. Once prepared and packaged, our delivery team takes over
    and arranges for timely delivery so that you get to enjoy fresh, tasty, homemade
    meals, served straight from the kitchen to your table. What makes us unique, you
    ask? Our food is made in small quantities and love and passion are the only additives
    that go into it
